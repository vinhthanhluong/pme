Lợi ích – Giá Trị

The Project Management Professional (PMP) certification exam can be demanding. Making time to prepare while balancing work and daily activities can be challenging. You may want to prep using an in-person PMP exam prep course but taking multiple days off is not the best option. Our PMP certification eLearning course includes:

  • 2000+ practice exam questions via our included exam simulator
  • Over 200 interactive study aids, like games and quizzes
  • Online access to Rita Mulcahy’s™ PMP® Exam Prep, 10th Edition book
  • Plus more valuable study aids!

Remember, there’s no set pace in an eLearning course. You can take a course in one sitting, or anywhere in between. Then, once you pass the test and get your certification, you’ll be able to take your career to the next level as a certified Project Management Professional!
